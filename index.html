Who We Are!

Shaheen New York Cricket Club is a vibrant and diverse cricket club founded by New York-born and raised players with deep roots in the Pakistani, Bangladeshi, and Indian communities. Established with the mission of uniting cricket enthusiasts from South Asian backgrounds, Shaheen NYCC embodies the spirit of cultural fusion, sportsmanship, and excellence.

Drawing from the rich cricketing traditions of Pakistan, Bangladesh, and India, the club offers a platform for players of all levels to hone their skills, compete in local leagues, and foster camaraderie both on and off the field. Located in the heart of New York, Shaheen NYCC not only promotes cricket but also celebrates the multicultural identity of its members, who proudly represent the next generation of South Asian-Americans contributing to the sport in the U.S.

With a commitment to community engagement and a passion for cricket, Shaheen NYCC aims to be a beacon for aspiring cricketers, providing a supportive environment where talent can thrive. Whether it's playing in local tournaments or organizing events, Shaheen NYCC continues to strengthen the bond between cricket and the multicultural community of New York City.
